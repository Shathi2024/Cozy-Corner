Our Story

AFRAN SHOHAN

Founder, CEO & Owner

Founded in 2025, Cozy Corner emerged from a simple dream of AFRAN SHOHAN, a former IT professional turned coffee artisan. His journey into the world of coffee began during his travels across Ethiopia and Colombia, where he discovered the magic of perfectly roasted beans and the warmth of local coffee communities. This inspiration led him to create not just a coffee shop, but a cultural hub in the heart of Dhaka.

Unique Coffee Experience

Under Shohan's guidance, we proudly offer our signature "Dhaka Sunrise Blend" – a unique fusion of Ethiopian Yirgacheffe and Colombian Supremo beans, roasted daily in small batches. Our cold brew is aged in oak barrels, giving it a distinct, smooth flavor that you won't find anywhere else in Bangladesh.

Our Community

From students finding inspiration over our Study Special lattes, to entrepreneurs closing deals over our signature Mocha, to artists sketching in our cozy corners – we've become a second home to Dhaka's diverse community. Every weekend, we host local musicians and poetry readings, making us more than just a coffee shop – we're a cultural hub.

Sustainability Promise

We source our beans directly from small-scale farmers in Ethiopia and Colombia, ensuring fair prices and sustainable practices. Our eco-friendly packaging and waste reduction initiatives have made us Gulshan's first zero-waste coffee shop, inspiring others to follow suit.
